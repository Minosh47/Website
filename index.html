About the Game

Stack & Smash is a simple, satisfying arcade game where you stack blocks as high as you can, then enjoy watching the whole tower collapse in a colorful smash.

The game is currently available on mobile and is updated periodically with new tweaks, bug fixes, and performance improvements.

Platform: iOS & Android (mobile)

Get Support

  • Your OS version (e.g., iOS 18.1)

  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if possible

Common Questions

The game crashed or froze.
Please restart the app and try again. If it keeps happening, email me with your device details and what you were doing right before the crash.

I made a purchase and something went wrong.
Send a screenshot of your purchase receipt and your device details to the support email above. I’ll look into it as soon as possible.

How often do you update the game?
Updates are released as needed for bug fixes, balance changes, and small improvements.

The game may use third-party services (such as analytics or advertising providers) to help understand how players use the game and to support features like ads or leaderboards. These services may collect device identifiers and basic usage data as described in their own privacy policies.
